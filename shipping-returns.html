Shipping & Returns

Everything you need to know about delivery and returns

Shipping Information

At Seoulie, we're committed to delivering your favorite Korean fashion pieces as quickly and safely as possible. We ship worldwide from our warehouse in Seoul, South Korea.

Shipping Methods & Delivery Times

Shipping Method Delivery Time Cost
Standard Shipping 7-14 business days $10.00 (Free over $100)
Express Shipping 3-5 business days $25.00
Priority Shipping 1-3 business days $40.00

Free Standard Shipping on all orders over $100! No code needed, discount applied automatically at checkout.

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed on the next business day.

International Shipping

We ship to over 150 countries worldwide. International orders may be subject to customs duties and taxes, which are the responsibility of the recipient. Delivery times may vary depending on your location and local customs procedures.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. Track your package anytime on our Track Order page.

Shipping Protection

All packages are fully insured and tracked. If your package is lost or damaged in transit, we'll send a replacement at no cost.

Delivery Issues

If you experience any delivery issues, please contact our support team within 48 hours of the expected delivery date.

Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love your Seoulie purchase! If you're not completely satisfied, we offer easy returns and exchanges within 30 days of delivery.

Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, items must meet the following conditions:

  • Unused & Unworn: Items must be in original condition with all tags attached
  • Original Packaging: Items should be returned in their original packaging
  • 30-Day Window: Returns must be initiated within 30 days of delivery
  • Proof of Purchase: Original receipt or order confirmation required

Sale items and final sale items are not eligible for returns or exchanges. Please check product descriptions before purchasing.

How to Return an Item

Step 1: Contact our support team at support@seoulie.com with your order number and reason for return

Step 2: We'll send you a prepaid return shipping label within 24 hours

Step 3: Pack your items securely in the original packaging

Step 4: Attach the return label and drop off at any authorized shipping location

Step 5: Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days

Exchanges

If you'd like to exchange an item for a different size or color, please follow the return process above and place a new order for your desired item. This ensures you receive your new item as quickly as possible.

Return Shipping

Return shipping is FREE for defective or incorrect items. For other returns, a $10 return shipping fee will be deducted from your refund.

International Returns

International customers are responsible for return shipping costs. We recommend using a tracked shipping service for returns.

Damaged Items

If your item arrives damaged, contact us immediately with photos. We'll send a replacement or issue a full refund.

Refund Policy

We process all eligible refunds quickly and efficiently. Here's everything you need to know about our refund process.

Refund Processing Time

  • Inspection: 1-2 business days after we receive your return
  • Refund Approval: Immediate upon passing inspection
  • Credit Card Refund: 5-10 business days depending on your bank
  • PayPal Refund: 3-5 business days

Refund Methods

Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for the purchase. We cannot process refunds to different payment methods or accounts.

You'll receive an email confirmation once your refund has been processed. Please allow your bank or payment provider additional time to post the refund to your account.

Partial Refunds

In certain situations, partial refunds may be granted:

  • Items returned without original packaging
  • Items with visible signs of wear or use
  • Items returned after 30 days but within 60 days
  • Return shipping fees (when applicable)

Non-Refundable Items

  • Gift cards and store credit
  • Downloadable products or digital content
  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Personalized or customized items
  • Intimate apparel and swimwear (for hygiene reasons)

Lost or Stolen Packages

If your tracking shows the package as delivered but you haven't received it, please check with neighbors and your building's front desk. If you still can't locate your package, contact us within 48 hours. We'll work with the shipping carrier to investigate and resolve the issue.

Still Have Questions?

Our customer support team is here to help with any shipping or returns questions
